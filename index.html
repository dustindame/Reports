Fantasy Auction Draft

A two-screen prototype for running a live fantasy football auction draft: a phone screen for logging picks and a TV-facing board for the whole room to follow along.

Phone

Player Entry

Search a player, tap the winning team, slide to the bid amount, confirm the pick.

 TV

Draft Board

All 12 rosters, budgets, recent picks, and a live news/odds ticker in one wide scrolling display.

 Phone

Team Picks

The page the Draft Board's QR code links to — pick a team, check their roster from your own phone.

Static prototype: picks entered on Player Entry are saved to this browser's localStorage and the Draft Board / Team Picks pages pick them up live in the same browser — a stand-in for a real shared backend (e.g. Firebase) that a production build would need. See README.md for details.
