Fantasy Auction Draft

A multi-screen system for running a live fantasy football auction draft: configure your league ahead of time, log picks from a phone, and follow along on a TV-facing board — with real-time sync across devices.

Setup

Draft Setup

Configure teams, budget, and roster/lineup ahead of time. Generates a shareable league code and commissioner PIN.

 Phone

Player Entry

Search a player, tap the winning team, slide to the bid amount, confirm the pick. Needs the commissioner PIN.

 TV

Draft Board

Every roster, budgets, recent picks, a real scannable QR code, and a live NFL news ticker in one wide scrolling display.

 Phone

Team Picks

The page the Draft Board's QR code links to — pick a team, check their roster from your own phone.

Multiple leagues can run side by side, each identified by a short shareable code — viewing a league only needs the code, but logging picks or editing setup needs the commissioner's PIN, checked server-side. If no league is selected, the app falls back to a local demo. See README.md for details.
